My passion for Development

Since I can remember I have been developing. Developing has given me an opportunity to express my creative side and at the same time challenge my logical thinking. It takes creativity to solve a problem. You have to orchestrate all the languages, their strengths and weaknesses, making a harmony.

I love what I do. I think what makes me so passionate about it is you are always learning. Especially when you try new things. So learn to make new mistakes, fail fast and keep moving. Sometimes it takes failing to learn how to be better.

My passion for UX and HCD

It wasn't too long ago when healthcare.gov was launched. It wasn't long ago when healthcare.gov failed. Not the idea, but the site. Healthcare.gov failed because they didn't know their audience, they did not research their users. For too long we have tried to serve the needs of others by assuming what they want. We need to meet people wherever they are.

HCD Process

INVESTIGATION

Here is the challenge
  • What do we know?
  • Who is impacted?
  • What do they need?

IDEATION

Knowing what we know
  • Do we understand what we know?
  • Is this tangible to do?
  • Can we prototype?

IMPLEMENTATION

User needs met
  • Can we builld these wireframes?
  • What are our MVPs?
